The popular artist is originally known as Emwah Wamington. He is from Jamaica and is noted world-wide for his work with Vybz Kartel, Nicki Minaj and the like.

Some of his top hits are:

  • Whap Whap
  • EastSyde
  • Coke

  • Deja Vu

2.Masicka-Dancehall

Masicka is a multi-talented, DJ, singer and song writer on the verge of breaking into the tightly knit circuit of the Jamaican dancehall scene.

Some of his top hits are:

  • Tyrant
  • Hit and Run
  • History
  • Fight for us
  • Most Wanted

3.Tate McRae-Pop

Tate Rosner McRae is a canadian singer, song writer, dancer and former actress Her major genre is pop where she has dropped various bangers.

Some of her top hits are:

  • Greedy
  • Exes
  • Run for the hills
  • You broke me first
  • Distant

3.Joeboy-Afrobeats

Joseph Akinwale is a Nigerian singer and song writer, currently signed to Warner Music Africa in partnership eith his record label Young Legend.

Some of his top hits are:

  • Sip
  • Nobody
  • Body and soul
  • Beginning
  • Don't call me back

4.SZA-Pop

Solana Imani Rowe is an American singer-songwriter.She first gained recognition through her self-released extended plays which helped her become the first female artist to sign with Top Dawg Entertainment.

Some of her top hits are:

  • Snooze
  • Kill Bill
  • Good Days
  • Love Galore
  • Broken Clocks
